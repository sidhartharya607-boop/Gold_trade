💡 Understanding Spread Arbitrage (Click to Toggle)

▼
🔹 What is Arbitrage? Arbitrage occurs when there is a pricing discrepancy (Spread) between two highly correlated Gold contracts (GOLD_PETAL and GOLD_MINI). This bot aims to capture profits when this spread deviates from standard levels.

🔹 Scenario 1: Spread Expansion (Spread is Low) When the spread is 850 or lower (undervalued), we execute: Buy Petal & Sell Mini. We expect the spread to expand back to its target of 1050, where we exit to book profits.
🔹 Scenario 2: Spread Contraction (Spread is High) When the spread is 1050 or higher (overvalued), we execute: Sell Petal & Buy Mini. We expect the spread to contract back down to its entry level of 850, where we exit to book profits.

🔹 P&L Calculation: Multipliers - GOLD_PETAL (Leg 1) has a multiplier of 100x (1 INR change = 100 INR P&L). - GOLD_MINI (Leg 2) has a multiplier of 10x (1 INR change = 10 INR P&L). - P&L is calculated automatically depending on the execution direction.
